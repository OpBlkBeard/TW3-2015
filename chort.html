Bestiary Description:Chorts are smaller than fiends, true. But still big enough to kill. -Agnes Thistle, herbalist from the Black Forest. Chorst are the somewhat smaler kin of fiends and bumbakvetches. Yet any witcher who thinks their diminutive stature means they present no danger commits a grave error- the kind that can end his career permanently. These denizens of dark and ancient woods are some of the most dangerous monsters known to man.
Location: Anywhere
Vulnerable Against: Devil's Puffball, Relict Oil
