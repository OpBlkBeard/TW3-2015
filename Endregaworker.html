Bestiary Description:I thought the workers were harmless. I thought wrong. — Janne the Gimp, trapper. Workers are the most numerous, and thus the most frequently encountered, caste of endrega. Within the colony their duty lies in building nests and cocoons, acquiring food and caring for eggs and larvae. When threatened they will summon warriors to aid them, yet if forced to defend themselves they will — and do so surprisingly well.
Location:Caves
Vulnerable Against: Golden Oriole & Insectoid Oil
