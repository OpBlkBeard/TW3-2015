Bestiary Description:Arachnids are lone hunters — they patiently wait for their prey to kill it with one swift strike when it appears. The same is true for the arachas, a huge creature that took a liking of the riverside forest, becoming its undisputed king. A ruler who does not tolerate other hunters on its territory. Including witchers.
Location:Caves and Forests
Vulnerable Against: Golden Oriole & Insectoid Oil
