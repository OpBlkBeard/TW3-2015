Bestiary Description:It just me, or is that gargoyle ogling us? — Lara Estevann, burglar from Loc Muinne Gargoyles are stone statues brought to life by magic in order to guard mages' laboratories and lairs from intruders. Their appearance alone has scared off more than one prospective burglar. Those who do not take fright at the sight of these horned and winged monstrosities usually die shortly thereafter, torn to shreds by stony claws.
Location: Old ruins
Vulnerable Against: Dimeritium Bomb, Elementa, Quen
